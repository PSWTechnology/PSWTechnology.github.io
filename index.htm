Sound and vibration monitoring

PSW-Tech presented a paper on "Automatic Wireless Sound Monitoring System and Its Application at Caldecott Tunnel" at Noise-Con 2011.

Our first product manages remote data acquisition. It publishes live sound and vibration readings wirelessly and automatically, and sends text messages to your cell phone upon critical events.

  • Multiple monitoring stations
  • Multiple channels per station, for sound and vibration
  • Publication of acquired data in real time
  • Instant notification of significant readings
    • on AJAX website
    • by Email
    • by text message
  • Wireless communication
    • Wi-Fi
    • cellular broadband
  • Continuous audio recording
    • Audio around exceedance events archived
    • MP3 clips accessible from web
